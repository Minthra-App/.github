## About Minthra

Minthra is building a platform for miniature creators who want better tools, clearer systems, and a stronger way to share their work.

The product combines structured tooling with creative expression: documentation, portfolios, publishing, and community-facing experiences designed to support the hobby without adding noise.

## What to Expect

- Public repositories when parts of the platform are ready to share
- Design and engineering work shaped by a clear brand and system language
- Documentation and examples aimed at builders, collaborators, and future contributors

## Current Status

There are no public repositories yet. This profile is the foundation for Minthra's public GitHub presence and will expand as external-facing work is published.
